COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease is entered into between Harbor Point Properties LLC ("Landlord") and
Riverside Cafe Inc ("Tenant").

Section 4. Term and Renewal. This Lease shall automatically renew for successive
one-year terms unless either party gives 90 days written notice of termination.

Section 7. Repairs. The Tenant shall be responsible for all repairs, including
structural repairs to the roof and foundation.

Section 9. Rent. Base rent is USD 4,000 per month and may be increased annually
by the Landlord at its discretion.

Section 12. Guarantee. Maria Gomez personally and unconditionally guarantees all
obligations of the Tenant under this Lease.
